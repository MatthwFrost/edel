# Chrome Web Store listing — Readel

The free, modern text-to-speech (TTS) extension for Chrome. Read aloud any webpage or article with natural AI voices. No sign-up required.

Readel is a TTS reader built for the web. Hover over text, hold Alt, and listen. That's it. No bloated UI, no menus, just one key.

## HOW IT WORKS

**Hold Alt** — hover over any paragraph and hold Alt (Option on Mac). The text highlights and Readel reads it aloud. Release to finish the sentence and stop.

**Double-tap Alt** — starts reading from that paragraph through to the end of the content. Press Alt again to stop immediately.

No buttons, no right-click menus, no floating widgets. The most minimal text-to-speech experience on Chrome.

## WORKS WHERE OTHERS DON'T

Most TTS extensions break on modern web apps. Readel doesn't:

- **Gmail** — read email bodies without copy-pasting
- **Claude and ChatGPT** — hear assistant responses out loud
- **Google Forms** — listen to responses and questions
- **Notion, Substack, Medium** — any reader-style layout
- Plus every blog, news site, Wikipedia, and article on the web
